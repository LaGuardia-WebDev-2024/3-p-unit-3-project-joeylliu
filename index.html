Recipe #1

  • Serves: 1 dubai chocolate
  • Time: 30 - 50

  • Serves: Churros
  • Time:25 - 45
Dessert Ingredients measurements
Dubai Chocolate Premium chocolate (milk or dark), a pistachio cream filling,
kataifi pastry for crunch, and tahini (with a chocolate bar mold)		 12 oz (340g) milk chocolate, 7 oz (198g) pistachio butter, 5 oz (142g) chopped kataifi,
and 2 tablespoons of tahini and unsalted butter
Churros A dough made from water, flour, eggs, butter, and salt, which is then piped and fried in vegetable oil 1 cup water, 1/4 cup butter, 1 tbsp sugar, 1/4 tsp salt, 1 cup all-purpose flour, 1 large egg, and 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Step 1: Melt the chocolate (save some chocolate for later) and spread the chocolate thinly on the mold. Place the mold in the refridgerator

Step 2: Toast the kataifi with butter.

Step 3: Add the pistachio cream mixture with the kataifi.

Step 4: Once the chocolate mold is ready, take it out of the fridge and put the kataifi on it.

Step 5: Once the chocolate is filled with kataifi, put the leftover melted chocolate on top of it and place the mold in the fridge once more.

Step 6: After around 10 minutes, the chocolate should be ready to eat!
